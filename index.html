Peanut Butter and Jelly

Let's learn how to make a peanut butter and jelly sandwich!

Ingerediants

  • Peant Butter
  • Raspberry Jam
  • 2 Slices of Bread

Instructions

  1. Lay out 2 slices of bread and spread peanut butter on the surface of one slice
  2. and then spread jam on the surface of the other slice.
  3. press the two spreaded sides against each other to form a sandwich and enjoy!
